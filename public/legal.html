Privacy Policy

How GEOrankHQ collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: May 26, 2026
Summary: We collect only what we need to provide the service. We don't sell your data. We use industry-standard security. You can delete your account and data at any time.

1. Who We Are

GEOrankHQ ("we", "us", "our") is a SaaS platform that helps businesses optimize their visibility in Google and AI-powered search engines. Our service is accessible at georankhq.co.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@georankhq.co.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: name, email address, and password when you sign up
  • Business information: business name, address, phone number, website, and category
  • Payment information: billing details processed securely by Stripe — we never store card numbers
  • Content you create: AI snippets, keywords, and schema markup you generate in the platform

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, and actions taken within the platform
  • Device information: browser type, operating system, and IP address
  • Cookies: session cookies for authentication and preference cookies for your settings

Information from third parties

  • Google: when you connect Google Business Profile or Search Console, we receive OAuth tokens and the data you authorize us to access
  • Stripe: payment status and subscription information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the GEOrankHQ service
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, alerts, password resets)
  • To generate AI-powered content on your behalf using the Anthropic API
  • To query AI search engines (Perplexity, OpenAI, Gemini) to check your business citations
  • To communicate service updates and important account notices
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data to train AI models, sell to third parties, or display advertising.

4. Data Sharing

We share your data only with trusted service providers necessary to operate GEOrankHQ:

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Anthropic — AI content generation (your prompts are processed but not stored for training)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Vercel — web hosting and CDN
  • Google — OAuth and API services (only when you explicitly connect)
  • Perplexity, OpenAI, Google — AI citation checking queries (business name and keywords only)

We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g. billing records for 7 years).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: request a copy of your data
  • Correction: update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: opt out of certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@georankhq.co.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption for data in transit, AES-256 encryption for data at rest, and row-level security in our database. We do not store passwords in plain text.

8. Children's Privacy

GEOrankHQ is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by email or in-app notice. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

Questions about privacy?

Email us at hello@georankhq.co and we'll respond within 48 hours.

Cookie Policy

How GEOrankHQ uses cookies and similar technologies.

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and understand how you use the platform.

2. Cookies We Use

Essential cookies (required)

  • Session cookie: keeps you logged in to your account
  • CSRF token: protects against cross-site request forgery attacks

Functional cookies (optional)

  • Preference cookies: remember your settings and UI preferences
  • Language cookie: remembers your language preference

Analytics cookies (optional)

  • Google Analytics: helps us understand how users navigate the platform so we can improve it. Data is anonymized.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Stripe may set cookies when you interact with payment forms. These are governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in to GEOrankHQ. Disabling analytics cookies won't affect your ability to use the platform.

5. Contact

Questions about our cookie use? Email hello@georankhq.co.
