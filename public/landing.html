// FAQ

QUESTIONS?
WE'VE GOT ANSWERS

What exactly is GEO and why does it matter now?

+

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization — the practice of optimizing your business to appear in AI-generated search responses. As tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google Gemini become primary discovery tools for consumers, appearing in their answers is as important as ranking on Google's first page. GEOrank handles both simultaneously.

How does the AI Citation Monitor work?

+

We send automated queries to ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google Gemini, Bing Copilot, and Meta AI based on your tracked keywords, then analyze whether your business appears in the responses. We check every 2–6 hours and alert you when your citation status changes.

Do I need technical knowledge to use GEOrank?

+

None at all. GEOrank is built for business owners, not developers. The schema builder generates copy-paste code, the AI snippets write themselves, and the action items tell you exactly what to do step by step. Most merchants are fully set up within 20 minutes.

How long before I see results?

+

AI citations can appear within days of publishing optimized content and schema markup — AI engines crawl and update frequently. Google rank improvements typically take 2–6 weeks, which is standard for any SEO work. Most merchants see measurable AI citation improvement within 2 weeks.

Can I use GEOrank for multiple business locations?

+

Yes — Growth plan supports 5 locations, Agency supports unlimited. Each location gets its own GEO score, keyword tracker, and AI snippet library. Agencies can white-label reports and manage all client locations from a single dashboard.

What's your refund policy?

+

We offer a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. If you're not satisfied within 30 days of your first paid month, we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.
